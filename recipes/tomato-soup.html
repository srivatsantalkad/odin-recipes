Tomato Soup

DESCRIPTION

This is a soup featuring tomatoes. Disclaimer: I made this recipe up, this is probably not how you make tomato soup.

INGREDIENTS

  • Water
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt

STEPS

  1. Boil water in pot
  2. Add salt to water
  3. Grind tomatoes in grinder
  4. Add tomatoes to boiling water
  5. Serve, add salt as necessary
