Pasta Salad

DESCRIPTION

A recipe of pasta salad. A dish featuring pasta, and salad. Disclaimer: I made this recipe up, this is probably not how you make pasta salad.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato paste

STEPS

  1. Boil water
  2. Add salt to boiling water
  3. Add pasta to water
  4. Optional: add any vegetables to your liking
  5. Drain water
  6. Add tomato tomato paste as needed
  7. Drizzle olive oil on pasta
