Oven-Roasted Asparagus

DESCRIPTION

This recipe: oven-roasted asparagus by swedishmilk, is described to eliminate the natural bitterness of asparagus and is instead supplemented by saltiness and the following ingredients.

INGREDIENTS

  • Asparagus
  • Olive oil
  • Clove garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lemon juice

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees Celsius
  2. Drizzle olive oil on asparagus
  3. Sprinkle garlic, salt, and pepper
  4. Arrange asparagus onto baking sheet
  5. Bake in oven for 12-15 minutes, sprinkle lemon juice before serving
